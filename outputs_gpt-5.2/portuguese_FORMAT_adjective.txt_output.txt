      applies: {}
      patterns:
      - pattern: "N + Adj"
        notes:
        - "Adjectives normally follow the nouns that they modify."
        examples:
        - "casa branca"
        - "campos verdes"
      - pattern: "Adj + N"
        notes:
        - "Reverse order is generally limited to poetic language."
        - "Some adjectives (e.g. bom, belo, grande) often precede the noun."
        - "Some adjectives can change meaning depending on position."
        examples:
        - "branca casa"
        - "verdes campos"
        - "um grande homem"
        - "um homem grande"
