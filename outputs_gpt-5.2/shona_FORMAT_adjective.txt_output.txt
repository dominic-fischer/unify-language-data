        examples:
        - "mùnhu asina kutaura."
        note: "Negation of verbal adjectives follows the nominal pattern, but the noun is replaced by a substantivized verb (infinitive)."

      note: "Adjectives can be intensified in two ways: suffixing -sa to the adjective stem or reduplicating the adjective stem."
