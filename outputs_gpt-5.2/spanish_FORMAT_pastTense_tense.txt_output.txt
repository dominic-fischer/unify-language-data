  Usage:
    es-preterite:
      note: "The preterite expresses past actions/events viewed as instantaneous or completed."
      examples:
      - "Ella se murió ayer."
      - "Pablo apagó las luces."
      - "Yo me comí el arroz."
      - "Te cortaste el pelo."
    es-imperfect:
      note: "The imperfect expresses past actions/states viewed as ongoing/continuing (non-instantaneous), focusing on the action in progress rather than its beginning or end."
      examples:
      - "Yo era cómico en el pasado."
      - "Usted comía mucho."
      - "Ellos escuchaban la radio."
